Our Committees

The Technical Committee is a giving committee as it consists of people who are willing to benefit everyone possible with all their effort
The PR Committee is responsible for creating, managing, and implementing the PR strategy of IEEE AlexSB. This includes dealing with media, VIPs, speakers, and sponsors to enrich the branch's image and position in the public eye
The Activities Committee name gives a pretty accurate description of it, As its volunteers participate in a wide range of activities and tasks throughout the season.
The marketing committee is responsible for spreading awareness of the organization's work and cause by creating marketing campaigns, promoting events, posting on social media, and holding marketing desks in onsite activities.
